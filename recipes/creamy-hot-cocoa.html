Creamy Hot Cocoa

Description

Simply melted, shaved, or grated dark, white, or milk chocolate combined with either milk or water.

Ingredients

  • ¾ cup white sugar.
  • ⅓ cup unsweetened cocoa powder.
  • 1 pinch salt.
  • ⅓ cup boiling water.
  • 3½ cups milk.
  • ¾ teaspoon vanilla extract.
  • ½ cup half-and-half cream.
